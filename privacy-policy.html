Privacy Policy for MealPlanner

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

MealPlanner ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MealPlanner mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Display name
  • Google account ID

User-Generated Content

We store the following data you create in the app:

  • Recipes and ingredients
  • Meal plans and calendars
  • Shopping lists
  • Family group information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your account
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Enable family sharing features
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication - for secure user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore - for cloud data storage and synchronization
  • Local device storage - for offline access

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared:

  • With family members you explicitly invite to your family groups
  • With Google Firebase services to provide app functionality

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase - Authentication and cloud storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini AI - Recipe import assistance (Privacy Policy)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, you can delete your account within the app settings or contact us directly.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete all your personal data within 30 days.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@lokosoft.com
  • Organization: Lokosoft
  • Location: Valencia, Spain

GDPR Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under GDPR. We aim to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your personal data.
